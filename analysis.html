How to Use This Tool

  1. Upload Your Data: Click the "Upload Assessment CSV" button and select your completed assessment file. The report will generate automatically, and assessment details will be populated.
  2. Add Analysis Details: Enter the names of the analysts and the date of analysis in the "Analysis Details" section. This is saved automatically.
  3. Review & Plan Actions: Click on each roadmap stage to expand it. For stages marked "Opportunity for Growth" or "Foundation Focus," accept the proposed action and add your own comments and specific next steps.
  4. Consolidate & Export: Once you're done, click "View Action Report" to see a summary. From there, or from the main page, you can export your reports to PDF for sharing. Both reports will include the assessment and analysis details.

Important Note: This tool provides a preliminary analysis based on a standardized logic model. Think of it as a first draft—it's essential for your team to review, challenge, and enrich these findings with your unique organizational context.

Optional: Co-pilot Analysis

For a deeper, AI-driven analysis of your results, you can use the Co-pilot. First, export your results using the "Export Results (CSV)" button From the maturity assessment, then open the Co-pilot and paste your data.

Note: This will open in a new browser window and requires a Co-pilot license to access.
